<div style="text-align: justify">
<p>To transpose a matrix, we need a new matrix in which the rows are the former
columns and the columns are the former rows:</p>
</div>

$$
A =%
  \begin{bmatrix}
    1 & 2 & 3 \\
    4 & 5 & 6
  \end{bmatrix};
   
A^T =%
  \begin{bmatrix}
    1 & 4 \\
    2 & 5 \\
    3 & 6
  \end{bmatrix}
$$
