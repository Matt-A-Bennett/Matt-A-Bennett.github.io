<p>The dot product is only valid for pairs of vectors (with the same number of
entries). You simply match up each element between the vectors, multiply them
and add the results:</p>

$$
\begin{equation}
u \cdot v =%
  \begin{bmatrix}
    1 \\
    2 \\
    3
  \end{bmatrix} \cdot
   
  \begin{bmatrix}
    4 \\
    5 \\
    6
  \end{bmatrix} = 32
\end{equation}
$$
